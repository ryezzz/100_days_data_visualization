The following visualization analyzes the green levels from 5,492 satellite images in order to see the relationship between vegetation, agricultural subsidies, GDP and productivity in Malawi, Africa.


It was created under the advisement of the UNDP Regional Bureau for Africa and with the use of Google Earth Engine.
